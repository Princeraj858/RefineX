About the Project
Smart Refinery Intelligence Platform
An AI-powered, full-stack operations management system developed by interns at IOCL Guwahati Refinery to modernize plant monitoring, maintenance, and decision-making.
Vision, Mission & Objectives
🔭
Vision

A Digitally Intelligent Refinery

To transform IOCL Guwahati Refinery into a fully data-driven, AI-augmented operation where every decision is backed by real-time intelligence.

🎯
Mission

Empower Operators with AI

Deliver an integrated platform that reduces downtime, prevents failures before they occur, and gives every operator actionable insights at a glance.

📌
Objectives

Measure, Predict, Optimize

Track KPIs in real time, predict equipment failures using ML, forecast production accurately, and provide a unified interface for all refinery operations.

⚠ Problem Statement

  • Equipment failures are detected reactively — only after breakdown occurs, causing unplanned downtime.
  • Production data is siloed across departments, making holistic analysis difficult for leadership.
  • Maintenance scheduling is largely manual and calendar-based, not condition-based.
  • Incident management lacks AI-powered severity classification and trend analysis.
  • No unified digital dashboard for all plant KPIs, safety scores, and energy consumption.

✅ Proposed Solution

  • ML-powered predictive maintenance using Random Forest & XGBoost on real sensor data.
  • Unified executive dashboard with all KPIs, trends, and AI-generated summaries.
  • Condition-based maintenance planner with work order management and calendar view.
  • AI incident severity classifier using TF-IDF + Random Forest / DistilBERT.
  • Interactive 3D Digital Twin of the refinery for visual plant health monitoring.
Key Benefits
⬆

Increased Efficiency

Real-time monitoring and AI insights improve plant availability and throughput by reducing unplanned stoppages.

🔧

Reduced Downtime

Predictive models identify failure risk 7–30 days in advance, enabling proactive maintenance scheduling.

🧠

Better Decision Making

AI Insights Engine combines all model outputs to give leadership a clear, prioritized view of what needs attention.

🛡️

Improved Safety

Safety KPI tracking, incident trend analysis, and automatic severity classification reduce risk and improve compliance.

💡

Innovation Culture

The Innovation Hub empowers every employee to submit ideas, vote, and contribute to continuous improvement.

🌱

Sustainability Tracking

Carbon emission dashboard tracks CO₂, water consumption, and energy intensity to support ESG goals.
